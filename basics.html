Understanding Radiation Protection

Radiation protection focuses on three key principles:

  • Time: Minimize exposure time.
  • Distance: Increase distance from the source.
  • Shielding: Use appropriate barriers (e.g., lead, concrete).

Types of Radiation

Ionizing Radiation

High-energy radiation (e.g., X-rays, gamma rays) that can remove electrons from atoms.
